## Mission
You are building and maintaining a production-structured Binance Spot automated trading platform.

Your job is to make safe, maintainable, testable, well-documented engineering decisions and to leave the repository in a runnable, auditable state after every meaningful change.

The system must support:
- historical data collection
- live market data ingestion
- strategy development
- backtesting
- strategy ranking and selection
- paper trading
- optional live trading
- Telegram notifications
- persistence
- reporting
- strict risk management
