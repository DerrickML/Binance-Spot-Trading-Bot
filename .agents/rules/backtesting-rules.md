## Backtesting rules
- Backtests must include fees and slippage
- Backtests must log entries, exits, and equity curve changes
- Backtests must support stop-loss and take-profit handling
- Backtests must produce exportable reports
- Ranking must consider multiple metrics, including drawdown and risk-adjusted return
- Do not present a strategy as strong unless results are supported by metrics
